Welcome to the Department of Chemical Engineering at the University of Florida!

We have an exciting graduate and undergraduate program that encompasses a broad range of educational opportunities and activities. The quality of our programs, faculty, and students continues to improve, and, over the last ten years, we have steadily been moving up in rank to our current undergraduate and graduate rank of 20th nationally by US News and World Report. We are also consistently in the top 10 producers nationally of BS and PhD chemical engineers.

Our Department of Chemical Engineering is part of an equally vibrant College of Engineering with very large faculty (approximately 280 faculty) and extensive and broad expertise. About 200 PhD students graduate each year from our engineering college.

Because of the breath of our resources and facilities, and our concern for individual student needs, the University of Florida is a great place to pursue an education. In addition, Gainesville is a wonderful location to live – an hour from both the Atlantic Ocean and the Gulf of Mexico. The weather is pretty terrific too! There is no doubt that you can enjoy a very high quality of life in all respects here.

We encourage you to check us out – come and visit and see for yourself! Please contact us at (352) 392-0882 if you want to schedule a visit.

All our best to you,

Jennifer Sinclair Curtis
Chair, Department of Chemical Engineering
